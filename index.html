Rock Climbing! (A very fun sport)


Now this is something you want to get into!

Rock Climbing is a recreational sport that has lately started to increase in popularity. You might even have heard of it before visiting this site! It involves at least one participant, but you can bring as many friends as you'd like. There are a couple different types of climbing, and you can find out about those, as well as the gear that you will need to get started, and where you can go to climb, in the corresponding links below, or on the navbar at the top of the page.
